Here is a description of the columns in the provided dataset:

Room: every entry stores a unique room number of the hotel room in which a guest is staying
Name: every entry stores the first name of a guest
Age: every entry stores the age of a guest in years
Expenditure: every entry stores the total amount of money spent by a guest on their previous meal in the hotel in rupees
Cuisine: every entry stores the type of cuisine selected by a guest in their previous meal
